About Betech

Betech is the annual technology and innovation event organized by BEST (Board of European Students of Technology). It brings together the students of UPM to engage in tech talks and competitions. Join us for an experience that blends learning, fun, and an unforgettable community spirit!

Tech Talks

Join industry experts and thought leaders as they discuss the latest trends in technology, AI, and innovation. Gain insights into the future of tech!

Team Design

Using only the provided tools and materials and your technical skills, to design and create a new item or a tool, which would fulfill the task requirements. Each year new and innovative tasks are presented, which always require teamwork, creativity and technical skills.

Hackathon

Having your laptop, your knowledge and limited time, you will have to solve a series of problems with your team by utilizing different tools and programs

Common Questions

What is Betech?

Betech is an annual event organized by BEST (Board of European Students of Technology) focused on bringing together students from across Europe to learn and connect over the latest developments in technology.

Who can attend the event?

Any student of Universidad Politecnica de Madrid can Participate

How long is each challenge?

Each challenge will last at least 6 hours long, excluding lunch and coffee break.

How many people are allowed in a team?

Each team shall have 4 members. If there is a solo registration, the organising team shall club you up with the other solo registrations.

Can I register individually or should we register as a team?

Yes, you can register Solo (individually). If you have a team, we advise you to register together.
